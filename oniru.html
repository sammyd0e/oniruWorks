Ejiwa Elegba Festival

The Ejiwa Elegba festival celebrates Olofin's heritage, culture, and tradition. It's a symbolic event offering prayers for peace and prosperity in Iru kingdom, the state, and nation.

Odun Isese Festival

The Odun Isese festival in Oniru Kingdom, Lagos, Nigeria, is an annual Yoruba traditional celebration held on August 20th, featuring traditions to honor deities and showcase rich cultural heritage.

5th Coronation Ceremony

The Oniru of Iruland, Oba Abdulwasiu Omogbolahan Lawal, reaffirmed his commitment to developing Iruland while preserving its rich cultural heritage on his 5th coronation anniversary.

Know more about Kabiyesi!

Oba Abdulwasiu Omogbolahan Lawal (Abisogun II) is the 15th Oniru of Iruland. Born on July 24, 1970, to Chief Taoreed Lawal-Akapo and Alhaja Muinat Olabisi Abeni Ajasa Lawal-Akapo, he was enthroned on June 7, 2020, after serving as Commissioner for Agriculture in Lagos State. He holds degrees from the University of Port Harcourt and the University of London, and has attended various prestigious programs, including Harvard University and the Wharton School. Read more
